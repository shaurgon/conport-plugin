# Pricing

ConPort is opening a public free tier with two paid plans on top. Pricing
is in **beta** — we're measuring real infra costs over the first
2–3 months and may adjust afterwards. Early subscribers are
**grandfathered**: the price you sign up at holds as long as the
subscription stays active.

_Last updated: 2026-04-15_

## Plans

| Plan | Price | Projects | Everything else |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | 1 | Full feature parity |
| **Basic** (beta) | $5/mo | 3 | Full feature parity |
| **Pro** (beta) | $10/mo | Unlimited (fair use) | Full feature parity |

**Feature parity is complete.** There are no Pro-only features —
everything in the app works the same on every plan. The only axis is
the number of projects you can own.

## Annual billing

Pay annually, get roughly two months free:

| Plan | Monthly | Annual |
|---|---|---|
| Basic | $5 | **$50/yr** (~$4.17/mo, 17% off) |
| Pro | $10 | **$100/yr** (~$8.33/mo, 17% off) |

## What's not in this table

- **No trial on paid plans.** The free plan covers that role indefinitely.
- **No per-seat or team pricing.** Team features are deferred — see the
  [roadmap](./roadmap.md).
- **No usage-based metering.** No per-request charges, no embedding
  volume limits, no "token" counters.
- **No enterprise tier.** Come talk in
  [Discussions](https://github.com/shaurgon/conport-plugin/discussions)
  if your use case is unusual.

## Beta period and price changes

The beta window runs for roughly 2–3 months after public free-tier
opens. During it:

- Prices may go up or down based on real infra cost data.
- Anyone already on a paid plan keeps their current price as long as
  the subscription stays active without lapse. This is not a promise
  for brand-new signups after the beta window closes.
- Any material change gets announced in
  [Discussions → Announcements](https://github.com/shaurgon/conport-plugin/discussions/categories/announcements)
  at least 30 days before it takes effect on new signups.

## Billing provider

Stripe handles all payments. We don't store card details — Stripe does.
If you're on a Russian card and need ЮKassa, let us know in Discussions;
we haven't wired it up yet.

All prices are in **USD**. Your bank converts.

## Fair use

"Unlimited" on Pro means "we don't put a hard cap on projects." If your
pattern looks like throwaway automation — e.g. hundreds of one-off
projects to bulk-generate embeddings we then have to pay for — we'll
reach out before doing anything, and throttle only if the conversation
doesn't go anywhere. In practice nobody normal will ever hit this.

## Refunds

Monthly plans are non-refundable but cancel-anytime — you keep access
until the end of the paid period. Annual plans get pro-rated refunds
for whole unused months within the first 60 days.
